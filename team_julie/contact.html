SAloha! Kamahao means wonderful, astonishing, remarkable. And that is just what you will find at the Marilyn Monroe(tm) Spa. Located on Kaanapali Beach in the beautiful Hyatt Regency Maui Resort, the spa offers full spa services in a lush environment right on the water. With our beachside cabanas, you can enjoy indulgent massages, signature body treatments and fabulous facials amidst the sounds and breaths of the ocean.

You can even take a little Marilyn with you by choosing from our fabulous line of signature Marilyn Monroe Spas products. The finest all natural, organic skincare, hair care, makeup, nail and lip colors are all available in the spa or to purchase online after you leave.

Getting to Maui

Posted by Julie on Feb 1, 2014

It's a short trip from The Big Island for those seeking to U N W I N D on Maui

Many guests fly to our beautfiul island of Maui from busy Honolulu International Airport (Code: HNL). What many don't know is, Maui's Kahului Airport offers direct service from the mainland. Guests wishing to avoid the stress of connecting through Honolulu International should consider a direct flights to Maui.

The sooner you arrive, the sooner you can U N W I N D.


The airport codes for Maui are:

  • Kahului Aiport (Code: OGG).
  • JHM West Kapalua Airport (CodeL JHM).
  • Hana Airport (Code: HNM).

    • There are many flights arriving daily, and the Hyatt Regency Maui operates a guest shuttle from Maui's airports. Contact the hotel for details.
